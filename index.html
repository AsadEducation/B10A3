Frequently Asked Questions

What unit types are available?

Studio to 3-bedroom apartments, offering 500-1500 sq. ft. with modern finishes, open layouts, and ample storage space.

What amenities does the building offer?

Residents enjoy a gym, rooftop terrace, co-working space, 24/7 concierge service, and secured access for all common areas.

What are the lease terms offered?

Flexible lease options of 6, 12, and 24 months are available, catering to both short-term and long-term residents.

Is parking available for residents?

Yes, secure underground parking is provided, along with bike storage and electric vehicle charging stations for residents’ convenience.

Is the building close to public transportation?

Yes, it's within walking distance to major bus and subway lines, making commuting convenient and efficient..

Are utilities included in the rent?

Utilities like water, gas, and trash are included. Electricity and internet are billed separately to residents.

What security features are in place?

The building has 24/7 security, key fob access, surveillance cameras, and on-site management for enhanced resident safety.
